Loft Tanks

Loft tanks are rectangular tanks that are mainly used for internal storage. We are the standalone manufacturers of loft tanks in the country spanning across a wide range of capacities. We have loft tanks in both stainless steel and plastic from 250L to 1000L that can be used for various indoor and outdoor purposes.
Advantages of Loft Tanks:
● They are compact in size, portable to use
● Made from high quality materials, safe for water storage
● Can be used for internal as well as external storage
● Suitable for places with height constraint
● Can be used for direct water dispensing in limited space
● Extra Wide manhole lids for easy cleaning (SS Tanks)
